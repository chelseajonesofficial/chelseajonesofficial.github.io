Behind the Song

my best friend casually dropped this absolute bar "we're so lucky to know about saturn". and that hit really hard. i'll never see saturn from up close, but it's beautiful to know it's out there. saturn is this beautiful soul that i almost had, but i lost her. I'm still so grateful to know she's there and to hope she's ok, but i'm forced to stay away. i wrote the song within a couple days, and texted the wonderful Grace Budge to record it with me. this is one of the most meaningful things i've ever done for myself. and now i'm sharing it with you. i hope you love it as much as i do
